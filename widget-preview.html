About Scott

I don't sell quick fixes or prescriptive solutions. I partner with people to get honest about what's actually going on, then build the capacity to do something about it.

I've worked with people managing careers, families, health challenges, and the particular exhaustion that comes from succeeding at everything except the things that matter most to them. The work is different every time. The approach is the same: start where you are, not where you think you should be.
